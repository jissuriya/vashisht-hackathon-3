How FoodBridge Works

🍽️

1. Post Surplus

🏢

2. Claim Needed Food

NGOs and food banks see exactly what's available and claim the food for their communities.

🚴

3. Delivery Partner Transport

Local delivery partners get notified and transport the food safely from donor to receiver.
